Note-taking has never been easier.

Use NoteGenius to maximize note-taking.

Using a laptop to take notes in class? Typing everything your professor writes on the board word-by-word? Say no more. NoteGenius is here. Simply take a picture of the board. Upload it using your phone. Annotate the text online. And more.

NoteGenius uses picture-to-text technology to convert your picture to text so that you can annotate it and be on top of things. It communicates real-time in order to maximize your learning experience so that you can succeed in school.
