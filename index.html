Snap. Annotate. Share.

Taking notes has never been easier.

Look up. Look down. Type two lines and delete the last five. It's an inefficient hassle to whip your head up and down between the board, the professor, and your laptop screen. At NoteGenius, we value the student experience of learning for life, not just studying for the next test. Driven by first-hand experiences and feedback, we've streamlined the note-taking process for students worldwide.

NoteGenius is a responsive, efficient, and automated approach to note-taking in modern classroom settings with the help of Optical Character Recognition (OCR), voice-to-text, and artificial intelligence technology. Simply snap a picture of the board; we'll take care of the rest.

Features

Amazing features, all available at your fingertips. Sign up today!

Snap.

Simply snap a picture of the board using your phone. We'll convert it into text.

Annotate.

Our AI assistant will automatically annotate the text. We'll locate the key terms and point you to useful articles, research, and websites.

Share.

Share the document with friends and classmates via email, Google Drive, and Dropbox or export to Word and PDF. Study together.
